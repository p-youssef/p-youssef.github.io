About

Architecture
by training.
Design by obsession.

I'm Jozian Sriek, an Architecture Engineer with a multidisciplinary design practice spanning UI/UX, graphic design, and video editing. I've spent the last several years leading design projects, conducting user research, and delivering creative solutions with tools like Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Revit, and AutoCAD.

As co-founder and Chief of Design at Credom, I lead a team handling everything from brand identity to user interface design. I also volunteer with Handize Architecture Academy, creating educational content for architects, and with Noqta, facilitating youth dialogue on social issues in Syria.

Fluent in Arabic and English, conversational in French and Italian — and always happiest where architecture, design, and storytelling meet.
