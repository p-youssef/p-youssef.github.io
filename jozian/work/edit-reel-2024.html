The Work

Editing is design — the same eye, applied to time

A motion and editing showreel pulling from a year of video work across Credom brand campaigns, social media content, and personal projects. The reel demonstrates the full range: tight social edits timed to music, documentary-style sequences, and branded short-form content.

Good editing is invisible — you feel the rhythm without noticing the cuts. The challenge in building a showreel is making those hidden decisions visible enough to demonstrate the craft without destroying the experience.

Approach

Music-led cutting, design-led motion

Every edit starts with audio — the music or voice track sets the pacing logic before a single cut is placed. Visual rhythm follows the sound structure, not the other way around. Typography and motion are treated as design elements: consistent with the brand system, never decorative for its own sake.
